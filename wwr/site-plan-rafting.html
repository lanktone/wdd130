Overview

Purpose

A well-designed website helps to establish and reinforce the rafting company's brand identity, reflecting its commitment to environmental sustainability, adventure, and outdoor exploration.

Audience

1. Who are the target customers? Adults looking for exciting outdoor activities, but are also conscientious about their impact on the ecosystem. 2. What do they expect to find by accessing the website? Potential customers want to make informed decisions when booking a rafting trip. A good website should include comprehensive information, such as trip details, safety information, booking and reservation instructions, necessary equipment and gear, FAQs, customer testimonials, and environmental commitments. 3. How will my audience usually access my site (desktop, laptop, mobile device)? The target audience is likely to access the website through a variety of devices, including desktop computers, laptops, and mobile devices such as smartphones and tablets. It will be essential to ensure that the website is responsive and optimized for various screen sizes and resolutions. In particular, travelers may use their smartphones to research and book outdoor activities while on the go.

Paragraph Font: Lora

Lora is a contemporary serif font with excellent readability for body text on digital devices. Optimised for screen appearance, it adds a touch of refinement to the website's design.
